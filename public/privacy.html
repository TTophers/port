Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here’s how we protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide directly such as your name, email address, and messages through our contact forms.

We also collect technical data automatically through cookies and analytics, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior on our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested services.
  • To improve and personalize your experience on our website.
  • To monitor and analyze site usage to enhance performance and security.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers that help us run our website and services (like hosting, email, analytics, and payment processing) under strict confidentiality agreements.

Examples include:

  • Supabase or database providers
  • Stripe for payments (if applicable)
  • Google Analytics or other tracking tools

We ensure these providers adhere to strict security standards and only use your data to provide their services to us.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect usage data. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may impact site functionality.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request data portability where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us via the contact page.

6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, please reach out through our Contact page.
