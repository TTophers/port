1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and messages submitted through our contact forms.

We do not retain or use any data collected through client sites. Any user data collected on sites we build remains solely under the responsibility of the site owner.

We also collect technical data automatically through cookies and analytics, including your IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior on our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and deliver requested services.
  • To improve and personalize your experience on our website.
  • To monitor and analyze site usage for better performance and security.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. However, to provide the services you request, we may share your information with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform (e.g., hosting, email, analytics, and payment processing). These partners operate under strict confidentiality agreements.

Examples include:

  • Supabase or other database providers
  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Google Analytics and other tracking tools

We ensure these providers follow strict security standards and use your data only to support our services.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect usage data. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect some functionality.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability, where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our contact page.

6. Security Measures

We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

8. Audience Notice

Our services are designed for adult professionals and businesses. We do not knowingly collect or process information from individuals under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, please reach out via our Contact page.
