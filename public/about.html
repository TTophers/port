About TOPH

Hey, I’m ChrisTopher Staples — a front-end developer with strong skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I specialize in building clean, responsive user interfaces and love turning creative ideas into code.

I’m also growing fast in back-end development. I’ve built API routes, connected Node.js logic to Supabase databases, and integrated Stripe to handle secure payment, all from scratch. Whether it’s authentication, data storage, or payment processing, I’m hands-on and always learning.

Outside of coding, I’m a music producer and sound engineer. I craft beats, mix/master tracks, and help artists bring their sonic ideas to life. You can check out some of my work here.

Whether you need a functional, fast-loading website or custom sound design, I bridge creativity and tech with intention. Let’s build something meaningful.
